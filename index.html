about us




At one of the most attractive locations in the city, at Strahinjića Bana Street, is the Dorian Gray restaurant. This premium restaurant has been working successfully for many years and with his quality food and pleasant atmosphere has won the hearts of many regular guests.

From the very beginning, this international cuisine restaurant has set high standards, both in the choice of dishes on the menu and in their aesthetic presentation. The menu changes discreetly from season to season and is constantly updated with new innovations, giving this place a world-wide breadth and authentic quality.
